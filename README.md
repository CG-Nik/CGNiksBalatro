A Balatro mod that adds a variety of random content that I felt like adding, with intention for the content added to be balanced.

Thanks to the people who help modders in the Balatro discord.
Special thanks to N' for making Vanilla Remade, which is an incredibly useful resource for making Balatro mods, and La Ginger for creating a very useful consumable template.

Lua and the Lua logo are owned by the Lua project. This mod isn't affiliated with or endorsed by the Lua project. This mod includes several unofficial representations of the Lua logo in pixel art in the sprites of several additions related to Lua cards. The original designer of the Lua logo is Alexandre Nakonechnyj, and you can find the official site for Lua at https://www.lua.org/.
